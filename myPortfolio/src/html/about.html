There were stretches where the day was filled with these sports from before school, to lunch, after school, and then when we got kicked out of the gym it was off to the park.

First Working Expirences

The first work I ever had was mowing my grandparents lawn each week. Sure this was a small job with family but there was no easy day. When I started driving the local supermarket was next in line to give me real work expirences. This would be the start of my retail life. It started small as I worked my way up from sacker to checker then head stocker.
